Games

I think that a person has to relax after studying very hard, so I would like to talk about some games that I would like to play. Hope you have the same interest with me.

A recently very famous game. The difficulty of it is not very high and we can enjoy the beautiful scenery in this game.

I only sometimes play it because I may lose myself in this game. It is too perfect except for the unreasonable character-raising system.

Arena of Valor is one of the world-famous games. A lot of people play it. The MOBA system and fighting are wonderful for me.
